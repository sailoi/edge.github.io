Getting Started

From Anywhere

Access your SSH connection from anywhere with internet access.

SSH Securely

Random connection routes and credentials are generated for each SSH session, and can only be used once. No resources are left open after the session ends.

Collaborate with teams

Easily and securely share SSH access with groups of users organized into teams, making collaboration with your teammates a breeze.

Open Source

What you see is what you get - our source code is transparent, giving you the peace of mind that there are no hidden or malicious codes that may compromise your application or edge computers.
